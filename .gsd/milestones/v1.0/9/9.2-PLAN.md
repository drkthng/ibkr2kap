# Plan 9.2: Option Engine & FIFO Integration

## Objective
Implement business logic to adjust the cost basis or proceeds of underlying stock trades based on the premium of the option that was exercised or assigned, and handle expirations.

## Context
- .gsd/SPEC.md
- .gsd/phases/9/RESEARCH.md
- src/ibkr_tax/services/fifo.py
- src/ibkr_tax/services/fifo_runner.py
- src/ibkr_tax/models/database.py

## Tasks

<task type="auto">
  <name>Enhance FIFO Engine for Option Events</name>
  <files>src/ibkr_tax/services/fifo.py</files>
  <action>
    - Create a mechanism or separate service (`OptionEngine`) to process `OptionEAE` records before standard stock matching.
    - For an `OptionEAE` event, close the corresponding Option `FIFOLot`.
    - If `transaction_type` == 'Expiration', realize the premium as a Gain/Loss in the `Termingeschäfte` pool.
    - If `transaction_type` in ['Exercise', 'Assignment'], link the option's cost basis to the underlying stock `Trade` generated on the same date/strike. Adjust the stock trade's `proceeds` or `cost_basis` accordingly.
  </action>
  <verify>pytest -v tests/test_fifo.py</verify>
  <done>Logic to modify stock trades with option premiums or realize expiration gains is implemented.</done>
</task>

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Expirations correctly generate realized gains/losses in the Termingeschäfte pool.
- [ ] Assignments and Exercises correctly fold option premium into stock cost basis/proceeds instead of realizing immediate PnL on the option leg.
- [ ] The pipeline seamlessly handles OptionEAE data end-to-end.
