  <action>
    Create `FXFIFOEngine` service. It needs to:
    1. Scan all ledger items (Trades, CashTransactions) chronologically that affect a foreign currency.
    2. Identify acquisitions (e.g. converted EUR to USD, or received USD dividends) and disposals (bought US stock with USD, converted USD to EUR, paid USD fee).

    4. Upon disposal, match against open FX lots, calculate `realized_pnl` in EUR, and compute the days between the lot's acquisition date and the disposal date to set `is_taxable_section_23`.
